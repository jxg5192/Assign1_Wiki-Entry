Meet the Friends!

Bella the Bunny

Bella is a soft, white bunny who loves hopping through the meadow and sniffing out tasty berries.

Personality Traits: Curious, gentle, caring, and thoughtful

Fun Fact: Bella can hop five times her own height when she’s excited!

Max the Squirrel

Max is a quick, bushy-tailed squirrel who loves climbing trees and finding hidden treasures.

Personality Traits: Playful, adventurous, energetic, and a little mischievous.

Fun Fact: Max once buried so many acorns that he forgot where half of them were!

Coco the Bluebird

Coco is a bright blue songbird who soars above the forest, keeping an eye on her friends below.

Personality Traits: Cheerful, helpful, observant, and optimistic.

Fun Fact: Coco loves to hum little tunes, and her songs often cheer up the whole forest!
