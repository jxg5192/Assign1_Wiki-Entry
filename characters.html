Meet the Friends!

Bella the Bunny

Soft, white bunny who loves hopping through the meadow and sniffing out tasty berries.

Personality Traits: Curious, gentle, caring, and thoughtful

Fun Fact: Bella can hop five times her own height when she’s excited!

Max the Squirrel

Quick, bushy-tailed squirrel who loves climbing trees and finding hidden treasures.

Personality Traits: Playful, energetic, and mischievous.

Fun Fact: Max once buried so many acorns that he forgot where half of them were!

Coco the Bluebird

Blue songbird who soars above the forest, keeping an eye on her friends below.

Personality Traits: Cheerful, helpful, and observant.

Fun Fact: Coco loves to hum little tunes, and her songs often cheer up the whole forest!
